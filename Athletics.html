About Me

Taekwondo

I joined Taekwondo in 2016 when I was in elementary school. What drew me to martial arts was the fact that I just thought it was really cool, and this mindset benefited me. It taught me discipline and helped me keep healthy.

I received my first degree black belt in Taekwondo on November 7th, 2020.

I am no longer taking Taekwondo classes as Taekwondo was quite time consuming and I wanted to focus more on my education.




Krav Maga

In the summer of 2022, I signed up for a Krav Maga class at Colorado Krav Maga, and instantly loved it. Krav Maga is a martial arts that is all about defending yourself from street attacks/fights.

I am currently an orange belt in Krav Maga and hope to improve my fighting skills even more!




Weight Lifting

I first got into weights during physical therapy after suffering an injury. So, in my sophomore year of high school, I took Weight Training 1. After the class I continue to work on improving my strength through weightlifting.
